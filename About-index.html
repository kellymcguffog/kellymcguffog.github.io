ABOUT ME
My name is Kelly McGuffog and I am from Melville, NY. I am the youngest in a family of four and we all have a great relationship with each other. I have an older brother, Jake, who recently graduated from the University of Maryland and I have a teacup peek-a-poo named Lola who I miss so much while I am away at school. I am a rising junior at Washington University in St. Louis. I am studying communication design and marketing and I am currently in the process of learning how to code using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I hope to one day apply my skills and make something of myself in the working world. In my free time, I enjoy spending time with my friends and family, traveling, and scrolling through social media. I especially love reading inspirational quotes to motivate me to work hard and strive to be the best.
