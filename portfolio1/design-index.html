Animals

Adobe Programming

This piece was created in Pictures for Communication. The task was to depict a bird, a cat, and an insect. I chose my three favorite breeds of these categories and used inorganic shapes to create a design that emphasize their most defining features. In the case of the peacock, I used this design element on its feathery tail, onn the butterfly, I highlighted its beautifully shaped wings, and on the tiger, I pointed depicted its stripes and claws.

Vitamin Package

Adobe Programming

This assignment was to create a brand name and produce the packaging for their new vitamin. After researching consumer tastes, I realized that most people prefer fruity tasting vitamins over any other flavor. Based on that, I decided to use a raspberry flavor and use a splash of raspberry juice to emphasize the 'fusion.'

Untitled

Adobe Programming

This book was created in Word and Image 1 with the use of InDesign and Photoshop. The circles that are portrayed in the book are meant to emphasize the repitition of her actions in Matt Mulican's poem, "Untitled." It also represent the overall circle of life and generations. The text moves down the page along three different columns to mirror this circle effect.
